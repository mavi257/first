Imagine wonderful, a place where nature beckons, where birdsong and blossoms fill the air, near Dhanalakshmi College of Engineering and Good Earth Residential School. SriGayathri Doctor's Nest showcases 700 villa plots including 32 beautifully designed duplex villas ranging from 1120 square feet. The development offers homeowners privacy and exclusively nestled amongst nature. With a wide selection of plots and 3 bedroom villas there will surely be something to suit your budget and household needs

  • Structure - R.C.C. seismic resistant Framed Structure as per Structural Engineers Design.
  • Walls - External Brick walls 9” thick & Internal Partition Brick walls 4.5” thick.
  • Plastering - Internal Cement plastering in ceiling and walls with smooth finish. External Cement plastering with rough finish.
  • Flooring - 24” x 24” Vitrified tile ﬂooring in living, dining, bedrooms , kitchen and balconies.
  • Main Door - Teak wood frame and shutters with Varnish finish.
  • Other Doors - Country wood frame and water proof flush door finished with enamel paint. PVC Doors for Toilets.
  • Windows - UPVC Frames & Glass Shutters. 10 mm square rods for grills finished with enamel paint.
  • Kitchen - Black Granite top for Cooking platform with stainless steel sink. Glazed wall tiles upto 2’0” high above the Cooking platform.
  • Dining - One wash basin with Tap connection.
  • Toilets - Indian / European water closets white colour of Parryware or any other equivalent brand. PVC Preci-tech Flush Tank, Shower, Cold water and Hot water CP fittings.
  • Electrical work - Three Phase Power supply. MCB Type Distribution board with concealed wiring and switches. A/C points with 15 Amps Metal Gland Plug & Socket for all Bedrooms. Sufficient and adequate Electrical points will be provided for Fans, Lights, Washing machine. Geyser & Exhaust Fan point for all Toilets will be provided.
  • Loft and Cupboards - Each bedroom will have one Loft & Cupboard provision of standard size.
  • Finishing - Internal Walls ﬁnished with wall putty and interior emulsion over putty. External walls finished with Exterior emulsion.
